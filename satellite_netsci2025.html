Networking and Clustering Network Scientists


The satellite Networking and Clustering Network Scientists aims to bring together co-organisers and participants of local chapters (national associations, research groups) of network scientists to share experiences and strategies to create, sustain, and expand collaborations on network science and promote research and education in network science within geographical regions.
Invited speakers and colleagues will share their experiences via short talks and a round table. Topics include: optimal organisation structure, strategies to attract and sustain active members, reaching diverse audiences, sustaining the network, organising scientific, educational, and cultural events, outreach activities, online vs in-person activities, sharing seminar mailist for cross-national collaboration, etc
The satellite will take place on 2 or 3 June, 2025, as part of NetSci 2025 in Maastricht, the Netherlands.
The satellite is free and open to officially registered participants of NetSci 2025. But if you are interested to participate, please, contact Luis Rochafor organisational reasons.
The satellite is sponsored by the Belgium Research Network for Network Science (BENet) and the Research Foundation Flanders, Belgium.
